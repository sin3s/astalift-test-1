At the Heart of the Formula

Nano-Astaxanthin

The reddish tone of the Jelly Aquarysta comes from Astaxanthin—an organic red pigment produced by microalgae. It is one of nature’s most powerful antioxidants and is very effective in boosting skin firmness.

Human-Type Nano-Ceramides

The human body naturally produces ceramides in the skin to keep it moisturized, but aging and cold, dry weather can cause a depletion and underproduction of ceramides. Jelly Aquarysta replenishes those ceramides, helping your skin regain its intrinsic beauty.

Nanotechnology

The results of the Jelly Aquarysta show so quickly because the ingredients – Nano-Astaxanthin, Nano-Lycopene and Triple Human-Type Nano-Ceramides have been nanolized by nanotechnology. These beneficial ingredients together with Tri-functional collagen penetrate deep into your skin on a cellular level and immediately work its magic on your stratum corneum.

How to Apply Astalift Jelly Aquarysta

We recommend washing your face with cleanser and then patting yourself dry with a towel before you begin using the Jelly Aquarysta to maximise its ability to boost your skin’s defense against ageing, UV damage, and free radicals.

Step 1

Using the spatula provided with the product, scoop a pearl-sized amount of Jelly Aquarysta and place it on your palm. Spread the product evenly on both hands.

Step 2

Press lightly on your cheeks and chin for 10 seconds with your Jelly-coated palms. Then, do the same on your forehead and eyelids. Repeat this step once more on your cheeks and chin.

Step 3

Proceed to the next part of your skincare routine. After using Jelly Aquarysta, your skin will be able to absorb the ingredients of the next product much better.
